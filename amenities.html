Our Amenities

Fully Stocked Bedroom and Bathroom

Enjoy a comfortable stay in our fully stocked bedrooms and bathrooms. Each room is equipped with plush bedding, fresh towels, toiletries, and everything you need for a relaxing stay.

Board Game Room

Our board game room is perfect for family fun or a friendly competition. With over 100 board games to choose from, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Heated Swimming Pool and Jacuzzi

Take a dip in our heated swimming pool or relax in the jacuzzi. Whether you want to swim some laps or unwind after a long day, our pool area is the perfect place to do it.

Staffing Always Available

Our friendly and attentive staff are always available to assist you with whatever you need. From restaurant recommendations to extra towels, we're here to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Private Living Room

Each bedroom comes with its own private living room, offering a cozy space to relax and unwind. Enjoy the comfort and privacy of your own living area during your stay.
