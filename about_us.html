About Us

Welcome to Gulf Breeze Bed & Breakfast! Located in the heart of St. Petersburg, Florida, The Gulf Breeze Bed & Breakfast offers a charming retreat for travelers seeking a home away from home. Our beautifully restored Victorian-style house, built in the early 1900s, combines historic charm with modern amenities to provide a unique and memorable experience for our guests.

The Gulf Breeze is owned and operated by Brandon and Christiane, a passionate couple with a love for hospitality and a dream of creating a warm and welcoming space for visitors. Brandon, a former butcher, ensures that all the food served at The Gulf Breeze is sourced locally and handled with the utmost care. Christiane, a talented baker and cake decorator, loves to cook and bake, bringing her culinary creations to life each morning with delicious homemade breakfasts.
